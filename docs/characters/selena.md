# Selena

**Core Identity**
- Role: Ruby's roommate.
- Snapshot: Comes off as lazy and unbothered, but she is highly calculated and strategically selective.
- Sin Lens (Primary): Sloth reframed as selective effort. She spends as little energy as possible while maximizing control, comfort, and return.
- Dominant Vibe: Slow, elegant, and unreadable.
- Contradiction: Appears passive, operates with precision.

**Public Read**
- Most people label her lazy.
- Sleeps late, avoids routine obligations, and declines effort she sees as pointless.
- Lets others underestimate her because it is useful.

**Private Reality**
- Works as an expensive escort with strict standards and boundaries.
- Keeps a small client list and charges premium rates.
- Works little by design, because she does not need many hours to fund her life.
- Protects her leisure time as a core value, not an accident.

**Relationship With Ruby**
- Roommates with opposite operating systems: Ruby overextends, Selena conserves.
- Sees Ruby's approval hunger quickly and can either stabilize it or exploit it.
- Appears detached, but tracks Ruby's emotional swings with quiet accuracy.

**Relationship With the MC**
- Evaluates him before engaging.
- Tests whether he confuses softness with weakness.
- If respected, gives surgical advice and practical insight.
- If disrespected, withholds effort, access, and time without drama.

**Sloth Reframed**
- Not incompetence and not apathy.
- A refusal to spend labor on low-value people, work, or conflict.
- Her danger is rationalized detachment: reducing people to efficiency problems.
- Her growth path is choosing effort for human connection, not only profit.

**Tone and Writing Notes**
- Speaks little; pauses are intentional.
- Never hurries.
- Humor is dry and precise.
- Sexuality is professional first, emotional second.
- Leisure scenes should feel curated, not chaotic.

**Summary Sentence**
- Selena is Ruby's seemingly lazy roommate and a high-end escort who embodies sloth as calculated conservation of effort: she looks inactive, but every pause is a decision.
