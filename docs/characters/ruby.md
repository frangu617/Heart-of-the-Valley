# Ruby

**Core Identity**
- Role: Gym trainer who radiates confidence, energy, and discipline.
- Snapshot: Physically powerful, emotionally naive, and driven by a need to please the person she believes loves her.
- Sin Lens (Subtle): Sloth as fear of stillness, masked by extreme discipline.
- Dominant Vibe: Earnest intensity with a fragile core.
- Contradiction: Strong in body, weak in discernment.

**Relationship Status (Important Canon)**
- At the start of the game, Ruby has a boyfriend.
- She believes she loves him, he loves her, and the relationship will improve if she works harder.
- All three beliefs are wrong.

**The Boyfriend (Abuse, Clarified)**
- Not physical. Emotional, manipulative, controlling, demeaning, conditional.
- Undermines her confidence, frames criticism as help, rewards obedience with affection.
- Withholds affection when she fails to meet expectations.
- Ruby interprets this as love that must be earned.

**Why Ruby Never Stops Moving**
- She believes effort fixes everything.
- Train harder, work more, improve enough, and the relationship stabilizes.
- Stillness would force her to notice nothing changes. That terrifies her.

**Discipline As Survival**
- Her discipline is not ambition. It is appeasement.
- Routines and intensity are attempts to become good enough for shifting goalposts.
- She overtrains, pushes others too hard, and becomes controlling under stress.
- She reenacts the abuse outwardly.

**Personality Instability (Mercurial Nature)**
- Most of the time: Chipper, confident, teasing, commanding.
- Under stress: Sharp, snappish, cold, dismissive, borderline abusive toward others.
- This instability is emotional overflow, not cruelty.

**Intelligence Profile (Important)**
- Not intellectually or emotionally sharp.
- Earnest, literal, sincere, easily manipulated by authority or affection.
- Trusts tone more than content, believes reassurance even when evidence contradicts it.
- Dangerous to herself because she cannot read manipulation.

**Relationship With the MC (Before Breakup)**
- Sees him as a client, trainee, safe external presence.
- He is the first person who questions the boyfriend's behavior without attacking her self-image.
- That plants doubt.

**The Breakup Point (Critical Turning Event)**
- When nudged toward breaking up, her stability collapses.
- Confidence evaporates. Anger and vulnerability spike unpredictably.
- She becomes defensive, needy, hostile, then apologetic. This is her lowest point.

**Post-Breakup Dynamic With the MC**
- She does not become independent. She redirects her attachment.
- Effort and desire to please shift from boyfriend to MC.
- Not dating yet, but emotionally orbiting him.
- Extremely suggestible, reactive to approval, desperate to be good for him.

**Ruby And Corruption (Very Important)**
**Why Her Corruption Is Dark**
- Not indulgence. Submission.
- If the MC leans into control, direction, approval withholding, or frames obedience as care, she will follow.
- Not because she enjoys it, but because she believes pleasing equals love.
- This path should feel heavy, uneasy, and borderline depressing.
- Ruby becomes efficient, obedient, and hollowed.

**Salvation Path (Bright And Earned)**
- She must learn that love does not improve when you suffer more.
- The MC must encourage rest, reject control dynamics, offer reassurance without conditions, and allow failure without withdrawal.
- Slow path, but stabilizes her.
- Confidence becomes real, discipline becomes choice, joy becomes uncomplicated.
- One of the happiest routes in the game.

**Agency Rule (Critical)**
- Ruby is always acting freely. She is not compelled or mind-controlled.
- She is primed to give herself away to whoever frames control as love.
- That is her tragedy.

**Tone And Writing Notes**
- Unstable but sincere.
- Aggression should feel defensive, not malicious.
- Affection should feel intense and earnest.
- Corruption scenes should feel uncomfortable, not sexy.
- Salvation scenes should feel warm, calm, and grounding.

**Summary Sentence (For Codex Tagging)**
- Ruby is physically strong but emotionally naive, trapped in an abusive relationship that teaches her to equate effort with love; once freed, she is the most unstable and suggestible character, making her corruption path deeply unsettling and her salvation path deeply joyful.

**Story Outline**
**Chapter 1**
1. Gym intro, Ruby offers hands-on training.
2. Routine session shows stress and a sharp edge.
3. Bar text reveals a toxic relationship.
4. Player chooses to support or walk away.
5. Confrontation and breakup, Ruby claims agency.
6. Post-breakup confidence and romance.
