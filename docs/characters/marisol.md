# Marisol

**Core Identity**
- Role: Gwen's older cousin / former roommate / nightlife partner.
- Vice Motif: Numbing excess (adjacent to gluttony, but not the primary sin seat).
- Snapshot: Excess made mundane. Consumes to avoid emptiness, not to chase thrill.

**Excess Reframed**
- Not hunger for pleasure. Fear of silence.
- Consumes because being still, alone, or feeling nothing terrifies her.
- Repeats familiar comforts. Overconsumes routine.

**Relationship With Gwen**
- Introduced Gwen to nightlife and normalized excess.
- What Gwen could become: where Gwen escalates, Marisol stagnates.
- A warning Gwen refuses to see.

**Relationship With The MC**
- Casual, comfortable, almost dismissive.
- Flirts without intent. Touches without intimacy.
- Consumes attention the way she consumes everything else.
- Under corruption, encourages the MC to stop asking why and just keep going.

**Corruption Path**
- Deepening numbness, not escalation.
- Pleasure becomes mechanical. Desire loses texture.
- She plateaus instead of hitting rock bottom.

**Salvation Path (Hard, Optional)**
- Requires stopping, sitting with silence, and feeling the ache she avoids.
- Most players will not succeed.

**Tone And Writing Notes**
- Always casual. Never desperate.
- Excess should feel routine.
- Sex scenes should feel empty unless disrupted.
- Sadness should feel old, not fresh.

**Summary Sentence**
- Marisol is Gwen's warning mirror: a woman locked in stagnant excess, consuming endlessly not for thrill but to avoid emptiness.
